About Us

Welcome to Wildlife Sanctuary, a dedicated haven for the conservation and protection of Kenya’s incredible wildlife. We are home to a diverse range of species, including the black-backed jackal, bat-eared fox, dik-dik, hyenas, cheetahs, elephants, and warthogs.

Our Mission

At Wildlife Sanctuary, we aim to:

  • Wildlife Conservation – Protect endangered species from poaching and habitat destruction.
  • Rescue & Rehabilitation – Provide care for injured animals and return them to the wild.
  • Research & Education – Study animal behavior and educate communities on conservation.
  • Eco-Tourism – Promote responsible tourism to support our wildlife efforts.

Our Wildlife

Our sanctuary provides a safe home for iconic African animals such as:

  • Cheetahs – The fastest land animals, known for their speed and agility.
  • Elephants – The giants of the savannah, shaping the ecosystem.
  • Hyenas – Spotted and striped species that play a vital role in the food chain.
  • Dik-Diks – Tiny antelopes that thrive in Kenya’s arid landscapes.
